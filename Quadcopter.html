Adjustments

After intially flying the drone, we were not fully satisfied with how it flew. The design still seemed lightly too heavy for quick ascenion and precise motion. To get a more satisfactory flight, we believe decreasing the weight of the frame or using more powerfully motors will solve this issue. We have opted to change the CAD design and have a new frame printed. Along with flight issues, after bringing our drone down to UIUC and setting it up for testing, we noticed a new issue where two motors were not functioning properly. These two motors were having trouble increasing their RPM. After resoldering the motors to different sides of our ESC, we have concluded our ESC was damaged during the process of moving.
