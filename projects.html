FERMILAB RADIOTELESCOPE

The Society of Women Engineers at the College of Dupage were given the task to create a device that could measure and output the voltage of a charging solar panel. This device would ensure the solar panel was functioning properly and monitored. Using an Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and elementary circuit analyis we were able to create such a device. My main role was helping write the Python code. For example I helped make the Raspberry Pi read the voltage and convert it from a 5 volt range to a 12 volt range. I also would trouble shoot the circuit and set up the device to work with a solar panel.
