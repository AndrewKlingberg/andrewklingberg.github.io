I am currently a junior studying Mechanical Engineering at UIUC. I am a first year transfer student and am looking forward to taking active roles in groups on campus. My current courses focus on thermodynamics, circuit anaylsis, modeling and design, and more.

College of Dupage



Associates Degree in Engineering Science

I am a graduate of the Engineering Pathways Program at the College of Dupage and recieved an Associate's Degree in Engineering Science in May of 2018. I was also a recipent of the Presidential Scholars Award and the Foundation's Achiever Scholarship. While at the College of Dupage I had an active role in the Society of Women Engineers and was apart of the Phi Theta Kappa International Honor Society and Engineering & Technology Club.
